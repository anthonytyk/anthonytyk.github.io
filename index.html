About Us

At ATET, we are committed to bringing companies and organisations accurate security recommendations across various services.

We are a team of experienced consultants and advisors in the cybersecurity domain.

Services

Security Risk Assessment

Security Risk Assessments helps to identify potential security risks and vulnerabilities in the system. Our assessment will provide an overview of your system's state by discovering its security weaknesses and addressing them early in order to save future costs from distruptions in the event of failed systems.

These assessments will also allow risk management to be planned ahead in order to mitigate adverse impacts from a security breach.

Security Posture Evaluation

Security Posture Evluation is crucial step for a company to identify the quality of their security features, as well as what is needed to strengthen their defences. Our evaluation allows your company to better plan your cybersecurity expenses to create a realistic cybersecurity roadmap.

Security Design Review

Security Design Review helps to determine if your product is designed to be secure and achieves security requirements indicated by several benchmarks and certifications.

Security Incident Assessment

Security Incident Assessment provides insights on past incidents in order to better enhance current security features to prevent relapses and provide efficient plans to react upon incident occurences..

Security Awareness And Training

Security Awareness and Training ensures that your staff are constantly trained on the best practices and security needs to ensure that the security profile of your organisation is well mainintend, and security policies are being performed.
